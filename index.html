About Us

Since 1946, BAEC has been a forward-thinking Built Environment Consultancy, continuously expanding our expertise and nurturing talent. With a multidiscipline team of over 1,000 professionals across 13 offices, we focus on quality, innovation, and growth. Our values—quality, reliability, proactivity, flexibility, and entrepreneurial spirit—drive us to deliver tailored solutions that add value to our clients and enable long-term success.

We help public and private sector clients transform the built environment with integrated expertise and a client-focused approach. By aligning our services with distinct needs, we provide expert counsel, hands-on support, and holistic solutions from conception to completion. Working flexibly and collaboratively, we bring the right skills at the right time, fostering trust and delivering exceptional results every step of the way.
